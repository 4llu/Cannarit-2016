Cännärit

YARR! Cännärit, the yearly event organized by computer science freshmen is back again! This year you will get to enjoy an adventurous atmosphere, as well as become one with your inner pirate in our rum-filled pirate themed party! The pirate ship lands on 16.11 at Bar Bäkkäri in center of Helsinki. From there it will continue to the after party at Smökki!

Join us and experience breathtaking sword duels, epic pirate music, and of course refreshments for all the thirsty bandits.
